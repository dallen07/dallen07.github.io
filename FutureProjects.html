Future Projects

Upcoming Projects that I have planned

Venture - explore various locations on Earth and "imaginary" realms hidden in the certain destinations

To the Ends... - 2.5D Maze game, traverse the maze from an angled-top view with different scenarios

Role

As of right now, I am drawing up concept art for the "Venture" game and still brainstorming on what it will all entail platforms it'll be ran on, what game engine will be used, and other things that will need to be consider to create a game.

Estimate Timeline

I would assume if I'm the only one doing everything and make appropriate time for it, I'll take a few years unless I go to school for game design, 3D modeling, digital art and 2D/3D level designing.

Technologies

For the concept, everything is hand-drawn on paper then will be transferred digitally. I will most likely use Photoshop, Unity/UnReal 4/Cry Engine or something of the sorts that is free or has a student account licensing.

Collaborations

I will definitely be looking for help and I do have people in mind to get me somewhere in the long-term but until I get my ideas more set-in-stone then I'll present them with something or might even ask them to give me ideas and how to refine my story and plot.
