Who is Drew Allen?

Bio

My name is Drew Allen, recent graduate from University of Michigan-Dearborn with my Bachelor's in Science for Software Engineering. Born and raised up from Southfield, MI and grew an interest in engineering through my father. Being surrounded by electronics and playing games, I wanted to learn how to program and "hack" into games. Over time, developed an interest in drawing, animating and music, I've decided to expand my knowledge on designing, animating, and programming.

Hobbies

I enjoy playing games, listening to music, drawing, dancing and watching anime. At around age 5, I was introduced to a Nintendo 64 and everything for me changed. Soon after, I got into playing instruments (I play clarinet, electric bass and electric guitar) and composing my own songs and wrote poetry. I grew up with Smooth Jazz, R&B, Hip-Hop and branched off to other genres when I had a radio show at University of Michigan-Dearborn. Through gaming, I was introduced to anime and RPGs (Role-Playing Games) where adventure and imagination were infinite and now I am in love with Japanese cartoons along with their culture, artwork and music.
